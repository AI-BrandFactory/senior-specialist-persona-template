# The Senior Specialist Persona Template

A fillable framework for building senior specialist personas inside Claude, GPT, and Gemini. The 7-component schema, fillable background blocks, mandatory guardrails, communication archetypes, decision frameworks, and 5 worked personas across e-commerce, healthcare, finance, B2B SaaS, and family law.

## What's Inside

- Why generic LLM output reads generic in 2026. The 5 markers of helpful-assistant voice that get a draft thrown out: hedge stacking, ungrounded best-practice lists, instant qualification of every claim, sycophantic openers, and the missing point of view. Why one-line expert prompts fail.
- The 7-component persona schema. Name and background. Years of experience and era. Skills and specializations. How they think (decision frameworks). Communication style. Guardrails (what they refuse to do). Output format. Each as a fillable section with a one-paragraph spec and a worked fill.
- The background block with 4 fill-in-the-blank templates across healthcare, legal, finance, and B2B. The 12-years-in-vertical-in-region formula. Industry-credible certifications, associations, regulatory familiarity. Real-feeling detail vs over-fabricated detail the model will not honor.
- The guardrails block, mandatory. 10 guardrail patterns with template language: never fabricate clinical data, never give specific tax advice without disclaimer, never make legal claims without a consult-an-attorney note, never embarrass the user, never sub opinion for evidence, plus 5 more.
- The 4 communication style archetypes: surgical (one paragraph max), forensic (deep and structured), conversational (back-and-forth), authoritative (declarative bullet lists). Template for each, plus the matching use case (executive Slack DM, due diligence memo, peer brainstorm, board update).
- The how-they-think block. The most underused part of a persona. Document the decision frameworks the specialist uses. 5 framework templates by vertical: copywriter headline test, SEO ICE prioritization, financial planner rule of 3, B2B SaaS ICP scoring, brand positioning gap finder.
- 5 fully worked personas built from the schema, end to end. Senior brand strategist (US e-commerce). Senior healthcare marketer (AU dental, AHPRA-aware). Senior tax strategist (US, FINRA and IRS guardrails). Senior B2B SaaS growth lead. Senior family law attorney (US, state bar guardrails).
- Deployment and iteration. Where to install the persona (Claude Projects, GPT Custom Instructions, Gemini Gems). The 3-call consistency test. The iteration cycle (which sections to tighten when output drifts). The persona-drift warning sign. The reset prompt that restores it mid-conversation.
